Example To Use Solve-Sudoku.js

Input determined cells of any Sudoku-Puzzle.
Leave undetermined cells brank.
Or click 'Load Example Puzzle 1' or '2' button to load one of examples puzzles.
To solve the inputted puzzle, click 'Solve!' button.
